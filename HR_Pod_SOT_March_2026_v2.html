Voice of the customer

What we're hearing from customer interviews

Two use case interviews completed. No demos shown yet — pure pain validation.

Kierra

Recruiter · Alt Health Insurance · ~8-person TA team

Strong signal

#1 Pain: Screening volume

500–700 resumes per entry-level role. Reviews every one manually — arrow key by arrow key. No AI screening in Jobvite.

#2 Pain: Scheduling coordination

Multi-step manual coordination across time zones via Teams. No Calendly access for hiring managers.

Key insight for us

Wants the product to be proactive and assistive — surface what needs attention, nudge on time-to-fill, help manage her day. Not just a pipeline view.

Harveen Singh

Global TA Partner · EdgeConnects

Cautious on AI

#1 Pain: Global scheduling

Scheduling across global time zones. Rescheduling caused by stale/unupdated calendars is frequent.

#2 Pain: Screening with human touch

Rejected AI screening tools for lacking human touch. Insists candidates deserve a real person in the process.

#3 Pain: Fraudulent AI applications

Candidates using AI to mass-apply with polished, generic resumes. Volume is up but signal quality is down.

Key insight for us

Compliance is non-negotiable and must stay human-driven. Open to AI for admin. Guardrails messaging lands hard here.

Across both interviews: Screening volume is crushing. Scheduling is a controllable bottleneck. Recruiters want AI to assist, flag, and prioritize — not decide. Candidate experience cannot be compromised. The “proactive assistant” gap Kierra flagged is not yet in our build or the customer-facing deck.

Gmail automation

🕶️ Ken D Dates — Screening Agent

Screens inbound resumes against role requirements. Populates AI Screening Score, Summary, and Matched Skills. Never auto-advances — recruiter decides.

🎯 Cali Anders — Scheduling Coordinator

Scans Google Calendar, applies panel templates and fatigue caps (4/week/interviewer), proposes optimal interview slots.

📧 Gmail automations (3-phase)

Sends available times → reads candidate reply → sends confirmation + calendar invite. Happy path only — no rescheduling logic.

⚠

Sourcing agent is not in this flow. No outbound integrations exist. When sourcing APIs ship, candidates enter at Step 1 with zero pipeline rework.
